I'll tell you how I became a Prince of a town called Rexburg
  1. Family

    I was born in Rexburg, Idaho on July 1, 2000. I have 3 brothers and 1 sister and am the youngest in my family. My family and I are very adventurous and love being outside. We go on a lot of ATV trips and we love playing sports/card games. My family keeps growing as I now have 4 nephews and 2 nieces. I grew up living in Rexburg and lived there until I was 19. After my 19th birthday I went a 2 year mission for my church to the Italy Milan Mission. After my mission I returned home and started attending Brigham Young University.

  2. Interests

    Throughout my life I have always loved playing sports. Anywhere from football to playing pickle ball to bowling. I love it all. Sports have always been a huge part of my life. Ever since I was little I was always involved in some kind of sport, mainly Football, Basketball or Track. (See my Football Video) I also really enjoy being outdoors and riding ATV's and hunting. Being outdoors and seeing the country is one of my favorite things to do because it is so calming and relaxing. I love watching movies and TV shows. Its hard for me to pick a favorite movie but it is safe to safe that my favorite TV show is The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air.
